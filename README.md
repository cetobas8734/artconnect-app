## üì¶ Overview

ArtConnect is a customer relationship management (CRM) platform for visual artists. This app helps you manage your artwork portfolio, track contacts, and visualize sales opportunities in an easy-to-use Kanban pipeline. Streamline your art business operations in one place.

## üé® Features

ArtConnect includes several key features designed to enhance your experience:

- **Artwork Portfolio Management**:
   - Easily add, edit, and organize your artworks.
   - Specify details like medium, size, and price for each piece.

- **Contact Tracking**:
   - Store and manage contacts in a straightforward interface.
   - Link contacts to specific artworks or sales opportunities.

- **Sales Pipeline Visualization**:
   - Use the Kanban board to track leads and sales stages.
   - Move opportunities through various stages easily.

- **User-Friendly Interface**:
   - Designed with usability in mind to make navigation simple for all users.

- **Cloud Storage Support**:
   - Keep your data backed up automatically using Firebase authentication.
